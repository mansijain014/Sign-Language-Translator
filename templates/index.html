About

There are over 460 million deaf people in the world and for many of them it is really difficult to learn the written language of their country, which means almost 80% of the deaf people worldwide are either illiterate or semi-literate. Hand Chat is an application which takes us a step closer to break these communication barriers with the help of Machine Learning & Deep Learning techniques. It can bring millions of such people who have or don't have hearing disability together, which will help us contribute to a fairer & more inclusive world.

Translate LIVE

Hand Chat brings you a LIVE Sign Language Translator which can convert American Sign Language Alphabets to English Text Alphabets in real-time. The model used will be improved over time to translate complete sign language gestures into text/speech in various other languages.

Read, Learn & Connect

Hand Chat consists of a Blog section where people can read & learn about various things related to sign languages & people with hearing disabilities. It is a good starting point if you're planning on learning any sign language. This again helps in removing the existent communication barriers.
